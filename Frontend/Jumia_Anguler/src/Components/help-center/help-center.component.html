How to Track Your Order

  1. Log in to your Jumia account
  2. Go to "My Orders" section
  3. Find the order you want to track
  4. Click "Track Order" for detailed status

You'll receive email/SMS notifications at each important stage of your delivery.

Cancel Order

How to Cancel Your Order

Before Processing

If your order hasn't entered processing, you can cancel it immediately from your account.

After Shipping

If your order has shipped, you can refuse delivery or request a return after receiving it.

Cancellation Steps:

  1. Go to "My Orders" in your account
  2. Find the order you want to cancel
  3. Click "Cancel Order" button
  4. Select your cancellation reason
  5. Confirm cancellation

Refund Information

Online Payments: Refund processed within 3-5 business days to original payment method

Cash on Delivery: Refund via bank transfer or mobile wallet within 5-7 business days
